Welcome to the Room Management Monitoring System (RMMS)

This platform is designed to help faculty, staff, and students of Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig efficiently monitor and reserve rooms across campus. View building layouts, reserve spaces in advance, and stay updated with real-time availability. Our system ensures streamlined room usage and better organization throughout the academic year.
